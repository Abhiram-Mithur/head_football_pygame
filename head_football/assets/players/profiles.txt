# Player Profiles

## Speedy
The fastest player on the field. Has excellent acceleration and top speed, but lacks power in headers.
- Speed: ★★★★★
- Jump: ★★★★☆
- Power: ★★☆☆☆
- Control: ★★☆☆☆

## Powerful
A strong player with powerful headers. Can send the ball flying across the field but moves slowly.
- Speed: ★★☆☆☆
- Jump: ★★★☆☆
- Power: ★★★★★
- Control: ★★★☆☆

## Balanced
A well-rounded player with no major weaknesses. Good at everything but not exceptional in any area.
- Speed: ★★★☆☆
- Jump: ★★★★☆
- Power: ★★★☆☆
- Control: ★★★☆☆

## Technical
A technical player with excellent ball control. Can place headers with precision but lacks raw power.
- Speed: ★★★☆☆
- Jump: ★★★☆☆
- Power: ★★★☆☆
- Control: ★★★★★

## Jumper
An acrobatic player with incredible jumping ability. Can reach balls that others can't.
- Speed: ★★★☆☆
- Jump: ★★★★★
- Power: ★★★☆☆
- Control: ★★☆☆☆
